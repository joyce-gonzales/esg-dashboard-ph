📖 What does Risk Level mean?
ESG Risk Level reflects how exposed a company is to environmental, social, and governance risks that could affect its long-term financial performance or reputation.
LowStrong ESG practices. Minimal regulatory, reputational, or operational risk from ESG factors.
MediumSome ESG concerns present. Requires monitoring — risks are manageable but not resolved.
HighSignificant ESG vulnerabilities — environmental violations, governance gaps, or labor issues that pose material risk to investors.
